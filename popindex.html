How does ComfyBot works?

Our application is a simple chatbot in which depressed people are welcome and open to share with us what they want to tell us about their experiences. It is available in the Google Playstore and the App Store for IOS. Quick navigation to ComfyBot: When you open our app, you will be directed to our dashboard, where you can click the button to say hello to us and continue your conversation with us. We will do our best to console everyone who is fighting!!!!!
